Sparkling Water

Description

What a nice drink to have: a tall cool glass of sparkling water. Especially if the day is warm and you are feeling flushed. But then again: maybe you feel too stuffy in that wintery sweater of yours. So have a refreshing drink.

This is not a recipe you say? Well I respectfully disagree. I think anything can be a recipe if you so wish. And if you appreciate simple things as they should be appreciated.

Do you?

Ingredients

  • A nice glass you enjoy
  • Water
    • Of the sparkling variety preferably
  • Excitement

Steps

  1. Take the glass.
  2. Fill it with water.
    • Cooled if you so like.
  3. Sip forcefully.
  4. Say Ouch because your throat hurts a bit.
