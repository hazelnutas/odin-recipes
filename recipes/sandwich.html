Sandwich

Description

A sandwich is a great thing indeed. It is both simple and complex. Both adventurous and safe. A real feast and a real friend. Like any healthy thing, it connects any opposites and makes them whole.

Ingredients

The shell

  • Bread
  • Maybe butter or a spread

The insides

  • Greens! Yum!
  • A piece of tofu fried in soy sauce
  • A strange sauce that a friend gave you last summer (they made it themselves)

Steps

  1. Prepare your shell by toasting the bread (or by taking it out of the bag) (or both).
  2. Do you want to spread something on the bread?
  3. Pile the ingredients:
    1. First the tofu.
    2. Or maybe the greens should go first? Are they supposed to be squished?
    3. Oh, you remembered you have a pickle in the fridge (or maybe your roomate does, but does it matter?). Let's add some.
    4. The mysterious sauce.
  4. Add the upper part of the shell.
  5. Squish a little.
  6. Enjoy!
