Snack Time

Description

Snacks. Who doesn't love snacks? I know I certainly do. So let's make an exuberant feast of it once in a while, shall we?

Ingredients

  • Fruits such as:
    • A fancy mango or such
    • Or mayhaps a strawberry
    • Why not a piece of lemon to freak people out and destroy your teeth too?
  • A cracker
  • A fancy spread
  • Also chocolate oat milk

Steps

  1. Take the snacks.
  2. Cut off anything you won't eat in advance.
  3. Pile them on a plate in a messy pile.
  4. Feast without restraint!

Et voila, your life is now enhanced a tiny bit.
